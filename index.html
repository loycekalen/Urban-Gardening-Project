👋 Hi! Let's talk Urban Gardening🏡

What is Urban Gardening?

Urban gardening is the practice of growing plants, fruits, and vegetables in urban areas. It can take many forms, from community gardens, rooftop gardens and vertical gardens to guerrilla gardening.

Learn more on Wikipedia

Tips and Tricks

  • Maximize your space
  • Use Compost and Mulch - practice kitchen composting
  • Choose the right container preferably Portable planters
  • Create an Irrigation System
  • Use dual-purpose plants
  • Research on plants and know your pests
  • Use high quality soil
  • Stick to minimalist gardening tools
  • Set goals you want to achieve
  • Have fun
  • Get creative
